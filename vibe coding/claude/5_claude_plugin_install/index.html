Chapter I

Where Light
Meets Stillness

There exists a place where time slows to a single note — a frequency felt rather than heard. It is not marked on any map, yet every traveler knows its pull. The horizon dissolves into haze; the earth breathes in long, patient rhythms.

Here, the ordinary falls away. What remains is the essential: light falling across stone, wind tracing the curve of a dune, the quiet dialogue between shadow and form. This is not a destination. It is a state of being.
